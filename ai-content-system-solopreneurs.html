3 Sample Prompts from the Guide

These aren't generic — they're engineered for the Content Tree system.

Blog Post → Email Newsletter
I've written a blog post. Now transform it into an email newsletter.

Blog post: [paste the full blog post]

Requirements:
- Subject line: create 3 options (curiosity / benefit / bold claim)
- Intro hook: more personal, like you're writing to a friend
- Body: 350-500 words — capture the core insight without reproducing the full post
- Include one personal aside not in the blog post
- Natural "read the full post" CTA
- Close: sets up next week's content

Output: 3 subject line options, preview text, full newsletter body.
Blog Post → 10 X/Twitter Posts
Extract 10 standalone X/Twitter posts from this blog post.

Blog post: [paste or summarize]

Mix of formats:
- 3 insight posts ("Most people think X. But actually Y.")
- 2 list posts ("3 reasons why [topic]:")
- 2 question posts (designed to get replies)
- 2 contrarian takes
- 1 personal story or confession

Each post must work completely standalone.
Max 280 characters. Label each by format type.
Monthly Planning Session Prompt
Help me plan my content month.

My business: [describe]
My audience: [describe]
Active platforms: [list]
Monthly posting target: [X posts total]
This month's business goals: [e.g., "launch new product," "grow email list"]
Last month's best-performing content: [describe what worked]

Create a content plan:
1. 4 pillar content ideas (one per week), scored by relevance
2. Each pillar mapped to derivative formats
3. 4 engagement-only posts distributed across the month
4. 2 promotional posts tied to business goals
5. Week-by-week calendar view
