Built by Entrepreneurs, for Entrepreneurs

We got tired of tools that looked great in demos but fell apart in the real world. So we built our own.

Our Story

Bevoza is a product of Chronic Internet LLC — a small, focused company based in Kapa'a, Hawaii, built by entrepreneurs who have lived the solopreneur life from the inside.

We know what it's like to manage a business from a laptop with too many browser tabs open, too many disconnected tools, and never quite enough time. We've tracked income across spreadsheets that didn't talk to each other. We've launched products without a real system and wondered why they underperformed. We've built content strategies in our heads and watched them evaporate under the pressure of a busy week.

Bevoza exists because we decided to solve these problems for ourselves — and then realized other entrepreneurs needed the same solutions.

Our Mission

Help solopreneurs and small business owners work smarter — with practical, AI-powered tools that are actually built for the way real businesses operate.

Not enterprise software with a free tier. Not generic templates with no context. Tools built from the ground up for the founder who wears every hat, has a limited budget, and needs things that work on day one.

Why AI?

We've been building with AI since before it was a buzzword. We've seen what happens when AI is layered on top of a solid framework versus when it's bolted on as an afterthought. Every Bevoza product ships with AI prompts that are specific, tested, and designed to extend what the template does — not just fill it with generic output.

Our philosophy: AI amplifies good systems. It can't replace them. So we build the systems first, then make them AI-native.

Where We're Based

Our team operates from the North Shore of Kauai, Hawaii. We believe that where you work shapes how you think — and building from a place of natural abundance, unhurried perspective, and deep focus has made our products better. We're not in a WeWork. We're in the real world, building tools we'd use ourselves.

The Company

Chronic Internet LLC was founded by Steve Monas, an author, entrepreneur, and builder who has spent years at the intersection of writing, business, and technology.

Steve has published multiple books on business and resilience, built several digital ventures, and spent years studying what separates operators who build sustainable businesses from those who stay stuck in hustle mode without systems.

Bevoza is the product library he wishes had existed when he started. Every tool in our catalog was built to solve a real problem from his own work — and refined with feedback from hundreds of entrepreneurs who use them daily.
